What is FrogChat?

FrogChat is a simple chat application that has but one purpose: to chat. It aims to tackle the many problems encountered on social networking sites these days, including unstability, overcomplexity and above all infringes on information confidentiality.

Why use FrogChat?

FrogChat is extremely fast, stable and easy to use. We don't work or affiliate in any way with government agencies. This means that your information is safe and your details private.
